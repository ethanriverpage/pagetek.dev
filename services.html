Services

What we bring
to the table.

Every engagement starts with understanding your business. From there, we build a technology strategy that fits — not a template we sell to everyone.

§ 01

IT Consulting

Technology decisions shouldn't be made in a vacuum. We work alongside your team to evaluate your current environment, identify gaps, and build a roadmap that aligns technology investments with business objectives.

This includes infrastructure audits, vendor evaluation, software advisory, and long-term planning. We act as your fractional CTO — the strategic perspective without the full-time overhead.

§ 02

Cybersecurity

Most breaches happen to businesses that assumed they were too small to be targeted. We take a proactive approach: risk assessments, security policy development, endpoint protection, email security, and employee training.

We follow Microsoft and NIST best practices to harden your environment against the threats that actually hit small businesses — phishing, ransomware, credential theft, and misconfigured systems.

"We build defenses before incidents happen, not after."

PageTek approach to security

§ 03

Computer Networking

Your network is the backbone of everything. We design, deploy, and manage wired and wireless networks built for reliability, performance, and security. From structured cabling to VLAN segmentation to Wi-Fi 6 access points — we handle the full stack.

For businesses with remote or hybrid teams, we also set up and manage VPN solutions, SD-WAN, and cloud networking to keep your distributed workforce connected securely.

§ 04

Backup & Recovery

Data loss doesn't announce itself. We implement backup strategies that follow the 3-2-1 rule — three copies, two media types, one offsite — with automated monitoring to make sure backups actually complete.

When disaster strikes, you need a plan that's been tested, not just documented. We build and validate disaster recovery procedures so your business can recover fast.

§ 05

Technical Support

When something breaks, you need it fixed. We provide responsive, hands-on support for hardware, software, printers, workstations, and the everyday issues that keep your team from doing their jobs.

Support is available on-site in the greater Atlanta area or remotely for quick-turnaround issues. No ticket queues that disappear into a void — just real help from someone who knows your environment.

"Real help from someone who knows your environment."

On-site & remote support

§ 06

Telecom­munications

VoIP systems, unified communications, and phone infrastructure that integrates with your existing tools. We help businesses move from legacy phone systems to modern platforms like Microsoft Teams Phone, RingCentral, or similar solutions.

We handle the migration, configuration, number porting, and training so your team is productive from day one.

§ 07

Information Security & Management

Beyond cybersecurity tools, your business needs governance. We help establish information security policies, data classification, access controls, and compliance frameworks relevant to your industry.

Whether you're working toward SOC 2 readiness, HIPAA compliance, or just need sane internal policies, we build the documentation and systems to back it up.

§ 08

Project Management

Technology projects fail when nobody owns the timeline. We manage IT rollouts, office relocations, cloud migrations, and system upgrades from scoping through delivery.

You get clear milestones, regular updates, and a single point of accountability. We keep the project on track so your team can focus on the business.
